Eric Bullock

Postdoctoral Research Scientist, Boston University. bullocke@bu.edu

My research focuses on advancing the use of remote sensing technology to tackle scientific questions related to land change monitoring and climate change. These issues include the role of forest disturbance in the terrestrial carbon cycle, the resiliency of mangroves to climate change, the effectiveness of protected areas, and applications for international greenhouse gas reporting mechanisms such as REDD+.
